El Idiota

ABOUT US

El Idiota is neither a writer nor a musical project, it is a binary system formed by Kamila Bartków and Diego Fabricatore for at least 4500 million years. Under our common gravitational center, different works of art take shape, which that may or may not contain brushstrokes of some of the numerous satellites and celestial bodies that orbit in complex and erratic orbits around this feverish and chaotic dance that still has a lot of fuel to burn.

THE IDIOTS

  • Kamila Bartków - Poland, (art). She expose rawness of the moods of a deep soul shown layer by layer. Like the wind you can feel her, but not hold her, is that she is music.
  • Diego Fabricatore - No Land, (writings; guitar). Foreigner everywhere, he only feels at home in the abysses of art, entangled in his own thoughts, sounds and silences.
  • Luis Figueroa - Bolivia, (vocals). He is born every day.
  • Generalle Torbilone Bonaparte - Italy, (drums). Lifting the spirit with his drums since Napoleonic wars, he now takes to the rhythm of this fervert struggle.
  • Daniel de Assis - Brazil, (bass). Bearer of a heart as big as his talent, with more than 30 years involved in music, he had the chance to participate in many bands and projects. Passionate about funk, soul, pop and rock, he makes music his therapy.
